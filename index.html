Welcome to the Neath

The neath is a place of darkness and decay, with evil lurking around every corner, but also of bountiful riches, wealth beyond imagine. Venture down into it's dungeons and free prisoners to help you with your plunders.

Neath is a strategy game set in a vast labrynth of underground dungeons and mazes. The gameplay is loosely based off of the roguelike style, also with strong influences from Dungeons and Dragons, Civilization and X-Com. The aim of the game is simple. Reach lowest section of the neath, and conquer it.
