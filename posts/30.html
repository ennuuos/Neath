Update 0.3.2.0 - These potions have bugs in them
Written by Travis on 14 June 2016

I've added more potions, specifically the penetrating sight and night vision potions. The first of which required me to fix a bug in which tooltips would not be displayed on world objects if a hero was selected. Well I fixed the bug and made it so the aforementioned tooltips only show up on chests and only when the hero has 'penetrating sight'. Night vision isn't actually working but the loot entity works.
